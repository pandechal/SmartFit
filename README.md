SmartFIT: Beginner guide to your fitness journey
System Description
The proposed system, SmartFIT, this app is designed specifically for beginners who want to start their fitness journey with guidance that is simple, personalized, and easy to follow. Using AI-powered tools, the app helps users build healthy habits, understand their bodies, and reach their goals efficiently.
________________________________________
Key Features
AI-Powered Workout Plan
●	Generates workout routines based on the user’s goals (fat loss, muscle gain, general fitness).
●	Adjusts difficulty based on the user’s current fitness level.
●	Provides step-by-step instructions and proper form.
________________________________________
AI-Powered Diet Plan
•	Creates personalized meal plans depending on dietary preferences (e.g., vegetarian, high-protein, budget-friendly).
•	Suggests simple recipes suitable for beginners.
•	Automatically adjusts based on calorie needs and fitness goals.
________________________________________
AI-Powered Macronutrient Counter
●	Analyzes meals and calculates macronutrients (protein, carbs, fats).
●	Provides recommendations to balance daily macro targets.
●	Helps users understand their nutritional needs in a simple way.

Calorie Intake Calculator

•	Calculates daily calorie needs using age, height, weight, activity level, and goals.
•	Tracks daily intake and compares it to the recommended target.
•	Helps users manage weight gain or loss .
